Settee 892

Vintage design settee designed by Vico Magistretti in 1960, the Carimate 892, released by Cassina. The settee is designed for Carimate Golf Club.

Characteristics

Beechwood frame, aniline or lacquer finish;
Sea grass seat;
Foam rubber seat. Fabric, vinyl, or leather upholstery.

Model - Details

To build the model of the sofa I made ​​several functions. Each function realizes a part of the settee. Most importanti part of the sofa are are made with the built-in function CUBIC_HERMITE. In this way I was able to model the most complex parts.
