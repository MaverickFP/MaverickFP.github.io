Chair 892

Vintage design chair designed by Vico Magistretti in 1960, the Carimate 892, released by Cassina. The chair is designed for Carimate Golf Club.

Characteristics

Beechwood frame, aniline or lacquer finish;
Sea grass seat;
Foam rubber seat. Fabric, vinyl, or leather upholstery.

Model - Details

To build the model of the chair I made ​​several functions. Each function realizes a part of the chair. Most importanti part of the chair are are made with the built-in function CUBIC_HERMITE. In this way I was able to model the most complex parts. The following images allow to compare the real chair with that made ​​by me.
